About us

Who are we?

AcubePi is a startup company, comprising of a team of skilled undergraduate students currently pursuing their Bachelor's in Engineering at Manipal Institute of Technology, Manipal.

What do we do?

AcubePi focuses on building and deploying chatbots for all kinds of websites, creating enhanced conversational experiences for end-users. We are developing our team in web development and design as well, thereby looking forward to expanding our line of work in these domains.

Our services

Chatbots on websites to make websites more interactive and user friendly.
